Warm Berry Compote

The Warm Berry Compote recipe from Allrecipes involves cooking frozen mixed berries with sugar, orange juice, and orange zest in a slow cooker until bubbling. A cornstarch and water mixture is then added to thicken the compote. This warm berry sauce can be served on its own or as a topping for desserts like ice cream.


Ingredients

  • 6 Cups Frozen Mixed Berries
  • ½ Cup White Sugar
  • ¼ Cup Orange Juice
  • 1 ½ Teaspoons Finely Grated Orange Zest
  • 2 Tablespoons Cornstarch
  • 2 Tablespoons Water

Steps

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in a slow cooker. Cover and cook on High until bubbling, about 1 1/2 hours.
  2. Stir cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved. Stir into berry mixture. Cover again and cook until thickened, 5 to 10 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.
